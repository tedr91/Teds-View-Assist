## Ted's View Assist

A ground-up View Assist frontend: pre-canned Home views (Clock, Handheld, Wall Panel), a consistent bottom navbar, a built-in background library, and Ted-styled assist overlays. Layers on the View Assist integration; requires Custom Icons (Fluent set) for the navbar.

> Install via HACS as an **Integration**, then add **Ted's View Assist** under Settings → Devices & Services. It downloads the dashboard, views, sentence blueprints, and backgrounds and registers them with View Assist (which must be installed and configured first). See the [README](https://github.com/tedr91/Teds-View-Assist#readme).
